How to use:

  1. Go to the Semmelweis University Neptun Portal and export your calendar in .ics format.
    Students: https://neptunweb.semmelweis.hu/hallgato
    Staff: https://neptunweb.semmelweis.hu/oktato
  2. Upload the .ics file and click convert.
